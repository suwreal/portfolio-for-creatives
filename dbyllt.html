Damn B*, You Live Like This?

A magazine showcasing the unique interior designs of various homes in Vancouver.

A four-week project, focusing on bold branding and photography. All photography done by me!

Special thank you to all the wonderful people who allowed me to photograph them and their homes.

The Concept

Behind the scenes.

Interior design magazines have always fascinated me and inspired my creativity. However, one thing that I could never help but notice is that all the furniture and homes in those magazines always felt unlived in. There was a lack of coziness that we typically associate with home. With that thought in mind and alongside the inspiration by the popular internet meme, "Damn Bitch, you live like this?", for my magazine class I decided to take a more authentic spin of interior magazines and showcase unique rooms from various people I knew in Vancouver.
